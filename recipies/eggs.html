The recipie is fairly simple and you will most likely have all of the ingredients in your home!

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Grease
  • Seasoning

Steps

  1. Season Flour
  2. Put Chicken in Eggs
  3. Shake Chicken in Flour
  4. Fry Chicken
